Microbiologist | Bioinformatician

My research focuses on Microbiomes, Pangenomics, and Metagenomics, with an emphasis on Comparative Genomics, Extremophiles, Archaea, and Plant-Associated Microbiomes.

About Me

I am a Microbiologist with a B.Sc. from Simón Bolívar University. Currently, I am a CAPES scholar pursuing a Master's degree in Genetics and Plant Breeding at São Paulo State University (UNESP). I am also an active researcher at CBioClima - Center for Research in Biodiversity and Climate Change. My work integrates genomics, bioinformatics, and molecular biology to explore microbial communities, with the goal of developing innovative and sustainable biotechnological solutions.
