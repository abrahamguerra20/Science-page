Research Projects

Comparative Genomics of the Nitroplast

Pangenome analysis of *Candidatus Atelocyanobacterium thalassae* (UCYN-A) revealed extensive metabolic reduction and a divergence that led us to propose two distinct species.

Revealing Two Species within *Methanobrevibacter smithii*

Using comparative genomics and ANI, we reclassified *M. smithii* into two species, also identifying antibiotic resistance and pathogenicity genes.

Genomics of *Lactiplantibacillus plantarum* HCA1

The genome of this strain, isolated from pig manure, contains multiple plantaricin and stress tolerance genes, supporting its probiotic potential for gut health and food safety.

Population Genetics of Aquatic Carnivorous Plants

This genomic study of *Utricularia* in Brazil and Colombia revealed geographic clustering and complex genetic structuring, highlighting high diversity and long-distance, avian-mediated gene flow.

Pangenome of *Candidatus Liberibacter asiaticus* (CLas)

A large-scale comparative genomic analysis of the causal agent of Huanglongbing (HLB) to identify key genomic features, diversity, and virulence factors.

[Ongoing]

Analysis of the *Halobacterium* Genus

A comprehensive analysis of the *Halobacterium* genus using phylo-pangenomic approaches to understand its evolutionary dynamics and adaptations to extreme environments.

[Results Pending]

Genomics of *Vreelandella venusta*

Genomic characterization of *Vreelandella venusta* isolated from the Galerazamba Solar Salterns to explore its metabolic capabilities and adaptations.

[Ongoing]

Halophilic Archaea from the Galerazamba Salterns

Isolation and characterization of halophilic archaea from a unique hypersaline environment to explore their biodiversity and biotechnological potential.

Usiacurí Mineral Water Wells

Assessment of the environmental potential and health risks of mineral water wells in Usiacurí, Colombia, through microbiological and chemical analyses.

Bacterial & Fungal Diversity in Oaxaca

An investigation into the soil microbial communities of a montane cloud forest in Oaxaca, Mexico, exploring the diversity and ecological roles of bacteria and fungi in this unique ecosystem.

Propagation of Ornamental & Succulent Plants

A horticultural project focused on optimizing propagation techniques for various ornamental plants and succulents, exploring methods to improve growth and yield.
